Connect through activities.


Have you ever wanted to go out and do something, but you didn't have anyone to go with, so you decided to just stay at home instead? Mingo helps you find people who like to do the same things you like to do that live around your area.

Mingo lets you find people who are like you. You can find people that are specific to the type of people you like to spend time with. For example, you might want to go play basketball with guys who are ages 25-30 who live within a 3 mile radius of your current location.
